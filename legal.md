## Personal Information We Collect

This site is informational. We do not run ads, analytics trackers, or store browser cookies. The only personal information we receive is what you choose to send via the contact form or email.

### Contact Form

- **What we collect:** Name, email, phone (optional), and message content.
- **Why we collect it:** To respond to your inquiry and plan follow-up work.
- **How long we retain it:** Until the conversation concludes or an engagement begins, after which it becomes part of the client record.

### Email

If you contact us directly at [{{ site.email }}](mailto:{{ site.email }}), we retain the email thread within our inbox provider for historical reference. We do not share these emails with third parties unless legally required.

## How We Use Information

- Responding to questions and scheduling consultations.
- Preparing proposals or scopes of work you request.
- Maintaining necessary records for active client projects.

We do not sell or rent personal data to anyone.

## Data Security

We store contact information in tools that offer industry-standard security controls. Access is limited to Milliarium AI personnel who need it to support you.

## Changes

We may update this page when our practices evolve. Changes will be posted here with a refreshed effective date.
